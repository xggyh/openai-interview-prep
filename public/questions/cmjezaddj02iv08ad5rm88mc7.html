Tell me about your management experience

People Management Manager 原题 ↗
Google · Manager · 📋 2 · 🕒 Early March, 2026

Problem Statement

Describe your experience managing people and teams. Share specific examples of how you've led, motivated, or developed team members.

我的思路与解法

题目本质

"Tell me about your management experience" —— Engineering Manager / Senior Manager 题。考察：leadership scope + style + impact。

类似 [[cm6ffodsw00013b6pt5izxwps]] (Tell me about your job and role) 但 angle 更聚焦 manager-specific experience。

回答结构（3-4 分钟）

Layer 1：当前 management scope（1 分钟）

具体数字：

  • N direct reports 各 level (junior / mid / senior / staff)
  • 是否带 manager-of-manager
  • Cross-team scope (跨多少 team / org)
  • 持续了多久

"I'm currently an EM at X company, with 9 direct reports — 6 ICs from new-grad to Staff, plus 1 TL who manages 2 more. Total org of 12. I've been a manager for 4 years, before that I was Staff IC for 5 years."

Layer 2：Management 哲学（1 分钟）

你的 leadership philosophy + 你怎么 derive 它。

"My approach: I optimize for team velocity over my own technical output. The single best leverage is in hiring + growing senior engineers who can take ownership.

Concretely:

  • 1:1 every 2 weeks (30 min each), strict no-skip
  • Quarterly career discussion — every IC has 1-3 year growth plan I track
  • Decentralized decisions — TL owns tech direction; I'm there for unblocking + cross-team alignment
  • Bias toward stretch projects — give people 20% more scope than comfort"

Layer 3：具体 impact + 故事（1-2 分钟）

最关键。Concrete examples 而非 abstract claims。

"Three highlights from past 2 years:

1. Hiring: I drove team from 4 to 9 in 14 months. Of 5 new hires, 4 promoted within first year. Hiring bar was tough — I rejected 80% of pipeline.

2. Promotion: Took my Senior IC from struggling on Staff promo to landing it Q3 last year. Worked with him on visibility (Tech Talks) + cross-team scope (drove platform migration).

3. Tough call: One IC was performance-managed out. I gave 6 weeks of structured feedback + PIP. He left with dignity and good ref. Hard but right for team."

Layer 4：Lessons & 不足（30 秒）

诚实 reflection。

"Two things I'm working on: (1) I default to coaching over direct feedback; I'm learning to be more direct when stakes are high. (2) I struggle with managing managers (TL on my team) — different muscle than managing ICs. I'm reading 'High Output Management' and seeking peer mentorship."

关键展示点

1. Scope numbers

报 direct reports + level distribution。让面试官 calibrate experience。

2. Philosophy + practice consistency

不只说"I value X"，要给具体行动证明你 live by it。

3. Concrete examples

3 categories 通常足够：hiring / promotion / tough call (或 performance management)。

4. Vulnerability

什么你不擅长？正在学什么？显得 self-aware 不假装完美。

加分项

  • Cross-functional impact：跟 PM / Director / VP 协作
  • Org change navigation：reorg / 裁员 / 战略转向时怎么 handle
  • Diversity & inclusion：specifc actions, not lip service
  • Failure recovery：你曾经犯过 manager 的错，怎么 fix

易错点

易错点 / Red Flag

❌ "I have a great team" — abstract，no signal； ❌ Only hire / fire 故事 — 缺 growing existing report 故事； ❌ 不提 specific scope numbers； ❌ 完美 / 没 weakness — 不真实； ❌ "I micromanage less" without showing how； ❌ 故事全 success — Manager 一定有 failure，讲一个。

核心要点

Manager interview 看的是：clear scope, articulated philosophy, evidenced impact, honest reflection。Phil's "Output of a manager = output of his team + output of teams he influences" 是经典 framework。

延伸 / Follow-up

Common follow-ups:

  • "How do you handle a poor performer?"
  • "What's the most important thing in 1:1?"
  • "How do you decide who to promote?"
  • "How big a team can you handle?"
  • "What's a hiring mistake you made?"
