## 题目本质

**"Project you are most proud of"** (People Management variant) —— Manager version of "most proud project"。参考 [[cm5ehcf6e059c838okykjrwwq]] 的 IC framework，但这里 angle 是 **Manager 维度的 proud project**。

## Manager-version 的差异

IC 版本：proud 通常关于 technical achievement / personal craftsmanship。

Manager 版本：proud 通常关于 **team building / org transformation / people impact**。

## 选 project 的 angles（Manager 视角）

- **Built a team from scratch** (hiring + culture)
- **Turned around 一个 struggling team**
- **Drove org-wide transformation**
- **Mentored someone who is now ahead of me**
- **Cultivated culture** (例 inclusion, technical excellence, work-life balance)

## 故事模板：从 0 build 7 人 team

**S**：2 年前我加入 X 公司组建新 ML platform team。空 org，没人 没 charter 没 infra。Director 说"build it"。

**T**：作为 founding EM 招齐 team + ship platform。

**A**:
1. **Hiring strategy**：6 个月招 7 人。我把 60% time 在 hiring。优先 senior IC bring tech direction，再 junior 围绕成长。Hire 标准明确：(a) ML 背景；(b) 平台思维；(c) Empathy。Pipeline 过 200+ resumes, on-site 30, hire 7。
2. **Culture**: Day 1 写下 team principles：(a) "Tools we build are for engineers to feel powerful, not blocked"；(b) "Disagree and commit"；(c) "Document over Slack"。每月 retro 看是否 living principles。
3. **Tech direction**：让 Staff IC (Day 2 hire) 主导 roadmap。我 facilitate + unblock，不写 design doc。
4. **Cross-team**：每月跟 PM org + Director sync。Surface team's progress + ask for resources。
5. **Growth path**：每 IC 有 1-3 年 trajectory。1 年内 2 个 promo (Mid→Senior, Senior→Staff)。

**R**：1 年后 platform 上线，3 个产品 team 使用，节省每 team 月 40 工时。Team 0 attrition。两个 IC promote。Director 把 ML platform charter expand 给我（add 6 people）。

## 为什么 proud（emotional reason）

Manager 维度的 proud usually：
- **Built something lasting** —— team / culture / norm 在你之后仍 thrive
- **Grew people** —— specific person 在 you mentor 下 became X
- **Team well-being** —— 0 attrition / high engagement scores
- **Personal stretch** —— 第一次 build org from 0，焦虑但 push through

> "I'm proud not because we shipped platform — that's table stakes. I'm proud because the team I built has the kind of culture I always wanted but never had. People take real ownership, debate respectfully, and lift each other. That's harder than ship."

## 关键展示点

### 1. People-centric story

不是技术 achievement。是 building team / culture。

### 2. Specific numbers

- N hires from N applicants
- N promotions
- Attrition rate
- Cross-team impact

### 3. Failure / struggle moment

故事不平铺直叙。提一次 hiring mistake / culture clash + 你怎么 navigate。

### 4. Lasting impact

不只是当时 ship，是 1+ 年后 team / 系统 / norm 仍 alive。

## 加分项

- **Mentor someone who is now your peer / above**
- **Failure & learning**：一个你 manager 时犯的错
- **Personal transformation**：你自己从 IC → Manager 转变中的 hardest moment

## 易错点

> [!pitfall]
> ❌ 选 IC project — 应该是 manager-centric；
> ❌ Take all credit (story 应该是 "team did X, I unblocked Y")；
> ❌ 没具体 person grew → abstract；
> ❌ 没 attrition / engagement 数据；
> ❌ "Everyone was happy" — 不真实；should have rough moments。

> [!key]
> Manager proud project = **lasting team / culture / individual growth that you catalyzed but didn't do yourself**。Multiplier impact > direct impact。

> [!followup]
> - "What's the hardest hiring decision?"
> - "Did anyone leave? Why?"
> - "If you had to redo, what differently?"
> - "How is the team doing now (after you left or scope changed)?"
